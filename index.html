Akan Naming Culture

In West Africa, newborns are given special names depending on the day they are born.

This is a web application that takes a user's birthday and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Get your Akan name by filling the form?
